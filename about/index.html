About This Site

kbits-theme-demo is a Pelican “blog” created to show off & demonstrate the various features & styles of the kbits-theme I created for my blog “Knowledge Bits”. The latter blog doesn’t use all of the theme’s features (yet), but even what it doesn’t use is still likely to be of interest to potential users of the theme. Thus, this site was created to demonstrate the theme in full.

Check out the “Features” category for articles on the various unique features of the theme, and check out “Examples” for samples of styles and standard Pelican features.

Made with Pelican and the kbits-theme
